Welcome to Dale's Slop Emporium, where we have been serving the finest slop in South Minneapolis since 2019! Here at Dale's Slop Emporium we transcend the norms of "plating" and "presentation" put forth by the restaurant industry. True to our name and mission, our food is neither liquid nor solid. It is slop. Our restaurant does not have plates or forks. If plates and forks are what you seek in a dining experience, you would be much more satisfied at another restaurant. If, however, you enjoy food that pushes the boundaries of both flavor and states of matter, look no further. You have found your culinary home!
